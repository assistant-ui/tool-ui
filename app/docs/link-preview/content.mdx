Copy [`components/tool-ui/link-preview`](https://github.com/assistant-ui/tool-ui/tree/main/components/tool-ui/link-preview) and the [`shared`](https://github.com/assistant-ui/tool-ui/tree/main/components/tool-ui/shared) directory into your project.
